Master Presenter

New age presentation skills



Join Workshops For Free

Workshop Overviews

COVID has changed the way we connect and Give presentations. It is time for one to learn and implement both the conventional and the digital methods in a presentation. Master presenter precisely serves the above purpose through 3-days workshop.

Why attend Master Presenter
  • New age storytelling
  • New age methods and techniques
  • New age tools
  • new age platforms
  • learn influencing through presentations
For Whom

marketing and sales professionals, leaders, students and practically anyone who wants to learn new age presentation skills.

USP

Learn creating presentations from scratch, hands on workshop, learn working on applications like PowerPoint, Google Slides, zoom, Canva and many more, creative storytelling methods, 3 opportunities to present and personal feedback from faculty.

Topics Covered

Do's and don’t’s of a presentation, conventional versus digital presentation methods, preparing content for presentation, formatting and aligning content, designing the content, delivering the content with new age presentation skills.
