Raja Rani

Lead a happy family life



Join Workshop For Free

Workshop Overviews

Raja Rani is a 3 day’s journey rather than a workshop for the married couples. Raja Rani will help the participating couples to choose the path called happy family life. join this journey to (re)discover, (re)frame and (re)design the life that which you dream to live.

Why attend Raja Rani
  • understand each other with a new lens
  • design the future together
  • open-up, heal, recovery with guidance
  • understand family in 2022 context
  • discover new meaning for family life
For Whom

  • married couples

USP

  • Get trained with nature at a hill station, Sessions in forest, Experiences of other couples, Personal 1 to 1 guidance, Life chart design.

Topics Covered

  • Family – 2022 Context, Man Vs Woman, Redesigning family, Identity Crisis & Coping Mechanisms, Upbringing Children, Work & Life, Family Vision.
